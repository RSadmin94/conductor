# Pricing Strategy

## Overview

Conductor offers three pricing tiers designed to serve projects from individual developers to enterprise teams.

## Pricing Tiers

### Starter - $29/month

**Target Audience:** Individual developers, small teams, side projects

**Features:**
- 5 projects per month
- Basic feasibility analysis
- Standard planning templates
- Email support
- Standard report generation

**Revenue Model:**
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Annual option: $290/year (2 months free)
- Target: 40% of user base

**Metrics:**
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC): <$50
- Lifetime value (LTV): $348 (12 months average)
- LTV:CAC ratio: 7:1
- Churn rate target: <5% monthly

### Professional - $99/month

**Target Audience:** Growing teams, agencies, mid-size companies

**Features:**
- Unlimited projects
- Advanced feasibility analysis with custom criteria
- Custom planning templates
- Priority support (24-hour response)
- API access
- Advanced reporting
- Team collaboration (up to 10 users)

**Revenue Model:**
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Annual option: $990/year (2 months free)
- Target: 50% of user base

**Metrics:**
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC): <$200
- Lifetime value (LTV): $1,188 (12 months average)
- LTV:CAC ratio: 6:1
- Churn rate target: <3% monthly
- Upsell from Starter: 30% conversion rate

### Enterprise - Custom Pricing

**Target Audience:** Large organizations, regulated industries, high-security requirements

**Features:**
- Everything in Professional
- Dedicated account manager
- Custom integrations (Slack, Jira, etc.)
- SLA guarantee (99.9% uptime)
- On-premise deployment option
- Custom AI model training
- Advanced security and compliance
- Unlimited team members

**Revenue Model:**
- Annual contracts: $5,000-$50,000+ per year
- Custom pricing based on:
  - Number of projects
  - API call volume
  - Support requirements
  - Deployment model
- Target: 10% of user base, 40% of revenue

**Metrics:**
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC): <$2,000
- Average contract value (ACV): $25,000
- LTV:CAC ratio: 12:1
- Churn rate target: <1% annually
- Sales cycle: 60-90 days

## Revenue Model Summary

### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) Targets

**Year 1:**
- Starter: 400 customers × $29 = $11,600 MRR
- Professional: 500 customers × $99 = $49,500 MRR
- Enterprise: 10 customers × $2,083 avg = $20,830 MRR
- **Total MRR: $81,930**

**Year 2:**
- Starter: 800 customers × $29 = $23,200 MRR
- Professional: 1,000 customers × $99 = $99,000 MRR
- Enterprise: 25 customers × $2,083 avg = $52,075 MRR
- **Total MRR: $174,275**

### Key Metrics

**Overall:**
- Gross margin: 85% (SaaS model)
- Net revenue retention: 110% (expansion revenue)
- Average revenue per user (ARPU): $65/month
- Customer lifetime value (LTV): $780 average
- Payback period: <6 months

**Conversion Funnels:**
- Free trial → Starter: 15%
- Starter → Professional: 30% (within 6 months)
- Professional → Enterprise: 5% (within 12 months)

## Pricing Psychology

1. **Anchor High:** Enterprise tier establishes premium positioning
2. **Decoy Effect:** Professional tier makes Starter appear more affordable
3. **Value Stacking:** Clear feature progression encourages upgrades
4. **Annual Discount:** 2 months free incentivizes annual commitment

## Competitive Positioning

- **vs. Manual PM Tools:** 3x faster project setup
- **vs. Generic AI Tools:** Specialized for project orchestration
- **vs. Enterprise Solutions:** 50% lower cost, faster deployment

## Pricing Adjustments

- Quarterly review of conversion rates
- A/B test pricing every 6 months
- Monitor competitor pricing
- Adjust based on LTV:CAC ratios
